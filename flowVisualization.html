Navigating the Visualization:-

  • Data Used: 042.csv
  • The Visualization on the left shows the fluid flow colored based on concentration
  • Use the Mouse or the Touchpad to rotate the cylinder along the respective axis
  • Use Scrolling inside the canvas to Zoom in or Zoom out
  • Use The Left and Right Arrow keys to move the cylinder in its respective direction inside the canvas
  • Press "A" or "D" keys on the keyboard to move the plane along the Z axis to view the flow at that point
  • Press "W" or "S" keys to move the plane faster
  • A scatter plot of the points matching the current plane position is shown on the right of the canvas
  • At any point in time, Press "R" to reset the plane position back to (0,0,0)
