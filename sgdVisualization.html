Navigating the Visualization:-

  • The Initial view is a Choropleth of Gun Deaths in the United States of America. The scale provided next to the map shows the number of deaths in that particular state. Darker the shade of Blue, greater the number of deaths in the state
  • Hover over each state to see an overview of deaths based on Gender
  • By Scrolling down, An overview of the deaths in USA are provided categorized by Gender and Age Group. This data shown is from the year Dec 2012 - Dec 2013
  • The size of the circle is scaled based on the count of deaths in that city. Also color coded: Male, Female
  • Hovering over the city will show an overview of deaths based on Gender
  • At any time while viewing the state details, hovering over an empty space in the state, provides overview of state details
  • Clicking on the state or any point outside the US map will zoom out and show up the original map
  • Once a city is selected, Scroll down to view the victim details grouped by state and categorized by Gender and Age Group
  • Details of every individual death is available below the overview, Males: and Females:
  • Males and Females are color coded based on the Age Group, this helps in knowing the deaths by Age Group
  • Age Group Details
    • Children: Ages 0 - 12
    • Teens: Ages 12 - 18
    • Adults: Ages 18+
  • Unknown Age group details are color coding using, Males: and Females:
  • Clicking on any city within a state will provide an the overview and details of the deaths in the city, similar to state
  • Hovering over individual details will show up basic details about the individual i.e Name, Age and Gender
  • Clicking on the icon will open a new window with a news website that provides more detail about the death
  • Disclaimer: Clicking on any of the city, populates the map with the deaths in the selected state, the deaths are normalized by the total deaths in that state (Population data is not considered).
